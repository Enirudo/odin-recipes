Pudim

A spoon along side a beatiful pudding on a plate (freepik.com)

Pudim, the brazilian pudding, a delicious and easy recipe to make.

Ingredients

Pudding

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 can of milk (measure of the can of condensed milk)

Syrup

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of water

Steps

Syrup

  1. Melt the sugar in the pan until it turns brown, add the water and let it thicken.
  2. Place in a round tin and pour the pudding mixture on top.

Pudding

  1. First, beat the eggs well in a blender.
  2. Add the condensed milk and milk and beat again.
  3. Bake in a medium oven for 45 minutes, with the round baking dish inside a larger one filled with water.
  4. Poke it with a fork to make sure it's well roasted.
  5. Let cool and unmold.
