Carrot Cake

A carrot cake with chocolate topping (freepik.com)

A delicious Brazilian cuisine dish mixing carrot, a vegetable, with chocolate topping. An exquisite flavor is produced by this excentric mix.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 4 eggs
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of cooking oil
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1 spoon of baking powder
  • 3 big carrots

Topping

  • 4 spoons of milk
  • 1 spoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup of powder chocolate
  • 1 cup of sugar

Steps

Dough

  1. In a blender, blend the peeled and chopped carrots, oil and eggs.
  2. Then add the flour, sugar and baking powder.
  3. Bake the mixture in a preheated medium oven (180ยบ C) for about 35 to 40 minutes.

Topping

  1. In a saucepan, bring all the ingredients to the boil.
  2. Pour over the cake while still hot.
